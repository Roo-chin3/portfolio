Hello, My name is Kaoru Sato Dugas. I aspire to create digital experiences that connect users in the U.S. and Japan.

I enjoy creating things, and I am passionate about solving real problems through design. I focus on crafting solutions that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful and functional for users.

My work centers on elegant, clean, and thoughtful design that helps businesses build products people can easily understand and enjoy. I believe that good design should simplify complexity and create intuitive experiences that feel natural.

With an understanding of different cultures, I strive to develop solutions that balance creativity with cultural sensitivity. This perspective allows me to design experiences that resonate with diverse audiences while maintaining a strong and consistent brand identity.

My goal is to create user experiences that feel seamless and engaging, while also helping brands stand out in a competitive landscape.

Outside of design, I enjoy traveling, practicing yoga, cooking and baking sweets, reading, and watching movies—especially suspense films. These interests inspire my creativity and influence my approach to design.
